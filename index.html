About Us

Creating unique experiences to bring smiles to people's faces.

Nintendo is a Japanese multinational video game company that has been around for over a century, having been founded in 1889 as a playing card company. The company later ventured into the video game industry in the 1970s, and has since become one of the most recognized names in the gaming world. y.

  • The release of the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) in 1985 revolutionized the video game industry and became the best-selling console of its time.
  • The introduction of the Game Boy in 1989 made portable gaming a mainstream phenomenon, with over 118 million units sold worldwide.
  • The success of the Wii console in 2006, which sold over 101 million units and introduced motion-controlled gaming to a wider audience, solidified Nintendo's reputation for innovative and accessible gaming experiences.

Nintendo's vision is to create innovative, high-quality entertainment that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. The company is committed to staying ahead of the curve with cutting-edge technology and pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the gaming industry.
